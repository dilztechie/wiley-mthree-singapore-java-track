When to Use?
** Requirements of the System are Clearly Understood
** Demand for an Early Release of the Product
** Software Engineering Team are not very Skilled or Trained
** High-Risk Features and Goals are Involved

*** Advantage: Software will be Generated Quickly during the SDLC
*** Disadvantage: It Requires Very Good Planning / Designing

*** Advantage: Flexible and Less Expensive
*** Disadvantage: Problems might Cause Due to System Architecture / Not All Requirements are Met

*** Advantage: Customer can Respond to Each Iterative

*** Advantage: Errors are Easily Identified
